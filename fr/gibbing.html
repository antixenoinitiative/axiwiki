Gibbing (Insta-gib)

Le Gibbing (ou Instagibbing) est un terme désignant l'utilisation d'une puissance de feu écrasante pour détruire un Intercepteur Thargoïd avant qu'il ne puisse réagir. By attacking a Thargoid with sufficient firepower it is possible to reduce the Interceptor’s hull to 0 faster than the health regeneration can heal. Typically this requires a large number of Pilots to all attack the Interceptor simultaneously.

It is also possible to Gib a Cyclops with a single CMDR, however this only works with 4x Large Shard Cannons and Premium Ammo.
